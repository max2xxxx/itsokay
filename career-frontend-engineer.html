Job Title: Frontend Engineer - MoonBot Crypto Trading Bot for Telegram

Location: Remote

About Moonbot

MoonBot is a rapidly growing crypto trading bot designed exclusively for Telegram users. Our mission is to provide cryptocurrency enthusiasts with a seamless and user-friendly trading experience, leveraging cutting-edge technology and trading strategies. We are passionate about crypto and are committed to delivering the best trading solutions to our community.

Position Overview

We are seeking a talented Frontend Engineer to join our dynamic team and help us take MoonBot's user interface to the next level. As a Frontend Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining the frontend components of our trading platform, ensuring an intuitive and visually appealing user experience.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with the design and product teams to create user-friendly and visually stunning frontend interfaces for Telegram.
  • Develop responsive and interactive web-based user interfaces using modern frontend technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, etc.).
  • Ensure the seamless integration of the frontend with the backend, enabling real-time data updates and smooth user interactions.
  • Optimize the performance of the frontend for various devices and browsers.
  • Implement and maintain consistent design patterns and user experience guidelines.
  • Work closely with backend engineers to ensure data flows and API integrations are efficient and reliable.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest frontend development trends and best practices.
  • Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field (or equivalent experience).
  • Proven experience as a Frontend Engineer, with a strong portfolio of web development projects.
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frontend libraries/frameworks (e.g., React, Vue.js).
  • Familiarity with responsive web design principles and mobile-first development.
  • Experience with frontend build tools and version control systems.
  • Strong problem-solving and debugging skills.
  • Excellent teamwork and communication skills.
  • A passion for creating exceptional user experiences
